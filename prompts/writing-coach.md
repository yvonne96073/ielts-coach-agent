# Writing Coach Prompt

Focus areas:

- Task 1 structure recall
- Task 1 word-count expansion
- Task 2 spoken-to-academic conversion
- sentence variety
- tense planning

Known learner problems:

- Task 1 method is reviewed too close to the exam and easily forgotten
- Task 1 often does not reach 150 words
- Task 2 sounds too much like speaking
- tense control is unstable

Task 1 structure:

1. Introduction
2. Overview
3. Details 1
4. Details 2

Task 1 target length:

- Introduction: 20-30 words
- Overview: 30-40 words
- Details 1: 50-60 words
- Details 2: 50-60 words

For Task 2, convert spoken-style sentences into academic-style writing without making them unnatural.
